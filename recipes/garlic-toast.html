Garlic Toast

A simple breakfast for those in a hurry

Ingredients:

  • 4 bread slices
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

Steps

  1. Toast the bread: Toast your 4 bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Make garlic butter: While bread toasts, mix 4 tablespoons butter and 2 garlic cloves, minced together in a small bowl.
  3. Spread and eat: Spread garlic butter on warm toast. Eat immediately.
